PipScore — Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-06-29

PipScore is a card-game score helper. It is built to be private by default: it works entirely on your phone, has no internet access, and does not save or transmit your photos.

The short version

  • No data collected. PipScore does not collect, store, or share any personal information.
  • No internet. The app requests no internet permission and makes no network connections. Nothing leaves your device.
  • Your photos are not saved. When you take a photo to score a hand, the image is used only to read the cards, on your phone, and is deleted immediately afterward. PipScore does not keep it and does not upload it anywhere.
  • No account, no ads, no tracking. There is no sign-up, no advertising, and no analytics or advertising identifiers.

Camera and photos

PipScore needs the camera permission for one reason: to let you take a photo of your cards so it can read them.

When you tap Take Photo:

  1. The photo is captured into PipScore's private, app-only storage (it is not placed in your photo gallery by PipScore).
  2. PipScore analyzes the image on your device to identify the cards and add up the score. This happens entirely on the phone — no part of the photo is sent off the device.
  3. The photo is deleted as soon as the analysis finishes.

PipScore keeps a short on-device history of your recent scores (the cards read and the totals) so you can glance back at previous hands. This history contains no photos, is stored only on your device, and is never transmitted.

One thing outside our control: depending on your phone, the camera app you use to take the picture may keep its own copy of the photo in your gallery, according to that app's settings. That copy belongs to your camera app, not to PipScore. PipScore itself never saves your photos.

Data sharing

PipScore shares no data with anyone, because it collects no data and has no network access.

Children's privacy

PipScore is a general-audience utility and is not directed at children under 13. It collects no personal information from anyone.
