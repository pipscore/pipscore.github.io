PipScore

An offline score helper for family & friends card games.

PipScore reads the cards in your hand from a single photo and adds up the points for you — no manual tallying. Built for family and friends casual card games like Swish, Swoop, Rummy, Golf, Cribbage, and Hearts, with adjustable point values so you can match your own scoring rules. Point your camera at your standard playing card hand and get an instant point total.

How it works: point your phone at a fanned hand, tap Take Photo, and PipScore identifies each card and shows the total. Set the point value for each rank once, and it remembers your preset.

Private by design

  • Works entirely offline — no internet permission, nothing ever leaves your phone.
  • No photos saved — the picture is used only to read the cards and is deleted right after.
  • No accounts, no ads, no tracking — no sign-up and no advertising identifiers.
